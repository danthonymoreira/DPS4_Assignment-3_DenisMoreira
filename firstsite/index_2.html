About Me

I am a fourth year Digital Arts and Sciences student at the University of Florida. My interests include Graphic Design, Design and Production, Audio Design, and Cultural Awareness.

I currently work as an intern for Teknology Solutions. Working on projects on health and fitness as a main video editor. I also work as a Videographer and Video Editor for the University of Florida's Dean of Student's Office.

In my spare time I have the privilege to serve as the University of Florida's Multicultural Greek Council President. Where I serve as the official voice of the council representing 14 different Multicultural organizations ranging from the Americas all the way to Southeast Asia.
